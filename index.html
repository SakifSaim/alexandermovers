OUR MOVING SERVICES

Offering reliable, efficient, and stress-free moving solutions tailored to meet your unique needs.

Local & Long Distance

At Alexandermover, we specialize in both local and long-distance moves within a 300-mile radius. Our expert team ensures that your move is smooth, efficient, and timely, with quick deliveries to your new home or office.

Residential & Commercial

We cater to both residential and commercial relocations, providing specialized services to meet the needs of homeowners and businesses alike. With our highly trained team, you can rest assured that your belongings will be packed, transported, and unpacked with the utmost care.

Full Packing Services

Our comprehensive packing service takes the stress out of moving. We send a team of professional packers to handle everything, ensuring your belongings are carefully packed and ready for a seamless transition to your new location.

Partial Packing Services

If you prefer to pack some items yourself but need assistance with the rest, our partial packing service is ideal. We provide high-quality materials and handle the delicate or bulky items, ensuring they are packed securely for the journey.

Unpacking Services

Settling into your new home is easier with our unpacking service. Let our experienced movers unpack and organize your belongings, saving you time and effort during the transition.

Labor Services

Our team provides expert labor assistance for residential and commercial moves. Whether you need help with loading, unloading, or rearranging furniture, our movers are ready to assist.

Furniture Assembly

We offer professional assembly and disassembly services for furniture during your move, ensuring that your items are safely dismantled and reassembled with care at your new location.
